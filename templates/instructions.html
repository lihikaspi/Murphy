How To Use Murphy?

Four steps to stress-test your strategy against the future.

1

Initial Plan

Enter your goal and execution plan. Choose the Murphy's law severity level ranging from Optimistic to Total Chaos.

2

The Scenario Maze

Review 3 critical obstacles from your failed future. Choose your preparation strategy for each threat.

3

Revise Strategy

Check out the new proposed strategy. "Like" or "Dislike" the identified weaknesses. Provide feedback to regenerate a more polished strategy.

4

Follow-Up

Follow the progress of your new revised plan. Receive a concrete checklist of tasks and "Traveler's Advice" to ensure this timeline succeeds.
